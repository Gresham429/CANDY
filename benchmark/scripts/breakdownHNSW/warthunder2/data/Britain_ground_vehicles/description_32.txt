Description of Cromwell_V_(RP-3): 
The Tank, Cruiser, Mk VIII, Cromwell V (A27M) (RP-3) (or just Cromwell V (RP-3)) is a premium rank II British medium tank 
with a battle rating of 3.0 (AB) and 3.3 (RB/SB). It was introduced in Update 1.59 "Flaming Arrows". Equipped with four 152 mm RP-3 rockets with a relatively straight trajectory, the Cromwell RP-3 could be played with more precision than its other rocket-bearing contemporaries like the T34 Calliope.
