Description of Crusader_Mk.II_(France): 
Several variants of the British Crusader cruiser tank family, notably the Crusader II and Crusader III, served with the Free French Forces. During the early stages of World War II, France was stripped of all equipment due to German occupation. The surviving forces received some of the Allies' tanks. The 1st autonomous tank company of the Free French Forces operated around 10 Crusader IIs and 18 Crusader IIIs.
