Description of AMX-50_(TO90/930): 
The AMX Char Moyen de 50 tonnes (TO90/930) is a premium gift rank V French medium tank 
with a battle rating of 8.0 (AB/RB/SB). It was introduced during Update "Apex Predators" as a reward for Battle Pass: Season X, "Royal Guard". An extremely deadly tank in the right hands, it can turn the tide of the battle against the enemy if played well.
