Description of XM1_(Chrysler): 
The Tank, Combat, Full-Tracked, 105-mm Gun XM1 (Chrysler) is a premium gift rank VI American medium tank 
with a battle rating of 9.0 (AB/RB/SB). It was introduced in Update 1.79 "Project X". The XM-1 (Chrysler) is an Xbox One exclusive vehicle, only available from the Microsoft store.
