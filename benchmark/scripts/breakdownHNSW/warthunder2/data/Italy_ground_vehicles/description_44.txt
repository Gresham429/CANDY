Description of M43_%22G.C.Leoncello%22: 
The Semovente 105/25 M43 G.C."Leoncello" is a premium gift rank III Italian tank destroyer 
with a battle rating of 3.0 (AB/RB/SB). It was introduced as a premium pack in Update 1.85 "Supersonic", and was discontinued after the 2019 Summer sales. It was made available briefly for purchase with Golden Eagles  during the 2022 "Italian National Unity and Armed Forces Day" mini-event.
