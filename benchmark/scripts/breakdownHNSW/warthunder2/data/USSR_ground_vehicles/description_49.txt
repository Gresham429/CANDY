Description of SU-76M_(5th_Gv.Kav.Corps): 
The SU-76M (5th Guards Cavalry Corps) is a premium gift rank II Soviet tank destroyer 
with a battle rating of 2.0 (AB/RB/SB). It was introduced during Update 1.49 "Weapons of Victory". The vehicle was made available during the WW2 Chronicles and "Tanker's Day Specials!" events in 2015, and again in the Victory Day Tournament and "Happy tanker's day!" events in 2017, it later appeared in the Warbonds shop as part of the trophy chest in April 2018.
