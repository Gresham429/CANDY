Description of BMP-2M: 
The BMP-2M is a squadron rank VII Soviet light tank (infantry fighting vehicle) 
with a battle rating of 10.0 (AB/RB/SB). It was introduced during Update "Starfighters". The BMP-2M is a light, mobile yet lethal light tank. Although its survivability and armour are not remarkable, its armament and utility are unmatched at its battle rating.
