Description of BT-7A_(F-32): 
The BT-7A (F-32) is a premium gift rank II Soviet light tank 
with a battle rating of 3.7 (AB/RB/SB). It was introduced during Update 1.89 "Imperial Navy" as one of the rewards for Operation H.E.A.T. It uses the same chassis as the BT-7 and as such, possesses the same armour, engine and crew layout but adds a new turret fitted with the 76 mm F-32 gun.
