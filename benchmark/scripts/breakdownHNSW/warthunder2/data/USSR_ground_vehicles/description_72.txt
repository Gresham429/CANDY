Description of Ph%C3%B2ng_kh%C3%B4ng_T-34_(USSR): 
The ▂Xe tăng phòng không 37 mm (English: 37 mm anti air tank), or ▂Phòng không T-34 (English: Anti air T-34) for short, is a premium gift rank III Soviet SPAA 
with a battle rating of 5.7 (AB) and 5.0 (RB/SB). It was introduced during Update 1.69 "Regia Aeronautica" as reward for the 2017 Operation S.U.M.M.E.R event.
