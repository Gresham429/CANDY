Description of KV-2_(1940): 
The KV-2 (1940) is a premium gift rank III Soviet heavy tank 
with a battle rating of 3.7 (AB/RB/SB). It was introduced during the Closed Beta Test for Ground Forces before Update 1.41. The KV-2 (1940) was a reward vehicle for the 2016 WW2 Chronicles and the 2017 Festive Quest events.
