Description of BM-13N: 
The BM-13N is a premium gift rank II Soviet tank destroyer 
with a battle rating of 2.7 (AB/RB/SB) also known as "Katyusha". It was introduced during Update 1.67 "Assault" as part of the 2017 Victory Day Event. It later returned as part of the Hot Tracks trophy and is currently only obtainable from different trophies or the marketplace via a coupon.
