Description of T-34E: 
The T-34E is a premium gift rank II Soviet medium tank 
with a battle rating of 4.0 (AB/RB) and 3.7 (SB). It was introduced as a premium pack in June 2014 during Update 1.41. and was removed from the Gaijin store in October 2016. It was also available in the Steam store from June 2014 to August 2018, when it was removed to become a reward for the 2018 "Operation S.U.M.M.E.R." crafting event.
