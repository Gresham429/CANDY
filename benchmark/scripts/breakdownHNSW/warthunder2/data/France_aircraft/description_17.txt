Description of D.371_H.S.9: 
The D.371 H.S.9 is a premium gift rank I French fighter 
with a battle rating of 1.7 (AB) and 1.3 (RB/SB). It was introduced as a premium pack in Update 1.73 "Vive la France" and was discontinued from the store after the 2018 Victory Day sale. It was a reward vehicle for the War Thunder's 7th Anniversary and War Thunder's 10th Anniversary events.
