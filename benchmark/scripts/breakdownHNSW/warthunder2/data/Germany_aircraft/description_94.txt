Description of Hs_129_B-2_(Romania): 
The Hs 129 B-2 (Romanian Air Force) is a premium gift rank III German strike aircraft 
with a battle rating of 3.3 (AB), 2.3 (RB), and 2.7 (SB). It was introduced as a premium pack in Update 1.41.27. This vehicle can no longer be obtained in the German Tech Tree but can now be found instead in the Italian tree as the Hs 129 B-2 (Romania) (Italy).
