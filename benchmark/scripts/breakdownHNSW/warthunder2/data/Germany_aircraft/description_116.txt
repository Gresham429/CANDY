Description of He_219_A-7: 
The He 219 A-7 is a premium rank IV German strike aircraft 
with a battle rating of 4.7 (AB) and 4.3 (RB/SB). It was introduced in Update 1.59 "Flaming Arrows". The He 219 A-7 is unique as it is the first user-created vehicle to be featured in the game as part of the Revenue Share Program, created by Daniil "Joy_Division__" Zaitsev, inspired by "NovA29R". The plane texture was created by Arthur Pacifica Carley. The He 219 A-7 is purchasable in-game with  and a percentage of the sale goes back to the model author who created it under the Revenue Share Program.
