Description of Tempest_Mk_V_(Germany): 
The ▀Tempest Mk V is a premium rank IV German fighter 
with a battle rating of 6.0 (AB), 5.7 (RB), and 6.3 (SB). It was introduced in Update 1.43 as a premium vehicle purchasable in-game with Golden Eagles , but was removed from in-game sale after the 9th Anniversary Sale.
