Description of Bf_109_E-7/U2: 
The Bf 109 E-7/U2 is a premium gift rank II German fighter 
with a battle rating of 3.0 (AB/RB/SB). It was introduced in Update 1.53 "Firestorm". It was a reward in the 2015 Thunder League and later became available as a trophy chest reward in the Warbonds shop in April 2018, but is currently a rare vehicle that can only be obtained through the Marketplace.
