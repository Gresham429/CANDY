Description of Magach_6R: 
The Magach 6R was a major modification compared to previous Magach 6s, featuring the same Blazer ERA but including a new AVDS-1790-2AG engine, with more electric power output when compared to the previous AVDS-1790-2A. Later modifications included the Magach 6R*, that prepared the vehicle to use the Nachal Oz fire control system.
