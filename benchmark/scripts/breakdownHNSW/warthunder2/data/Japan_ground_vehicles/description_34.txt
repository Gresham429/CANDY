Description of M41A1_(Japan): 
The Light Tank M41 SDF was part of the second defense build-up plan, introduced to enhance the self defense capability of the JSDF. Japan also developed its own domestic tanks, such as the Type 61 MBT and the Type 60 APC. The M41s were supplied by the Americans to replace the obsolete M24, while the Type 61 took over the role of the M4A3 (76) W.
