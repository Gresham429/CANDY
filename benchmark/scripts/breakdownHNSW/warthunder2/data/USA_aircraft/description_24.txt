Description of Rasmussen%27s_P-36A: 
Rasmussen's P-36A Hawk is a gift rank I American fighter 
with a battle rating of 1.3 (AB/RB) and 1.7 (SB). It has been in the game since the start of the Open Beta Test prior to Update 1.27. Rasmussen's P-36A Hawk is a unique American low-tier aircraft in War Thunder. Although it does not appear in the American tech tree, it can be purchased in the Gaijin store.
