Description of Tiger_II_(H): 
The Pz.Kpfw. VI Ausf. B (H) (Sd.Kfz. Index: Sd.Kfz. 182) is a rank IV German heavy tank 
with a battle rating of 7.0 (AB) and 6.7 (RB/SB). It was introduced during the Closed Beta Test for Ground Forces before Update 1.41. The Tiger II (H) remains one of the more potent foes a player could face in the Rank IV battles.
