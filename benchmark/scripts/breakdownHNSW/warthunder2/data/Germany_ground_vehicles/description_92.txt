Description of Pz.Bef.Wg.VI_P: 
The Panzerbefehlswagen VI P is a premium rank IV German heavy tank 
with a battle rating of 5.7 (AB/RB/SB). It was introduced in Update 1.45 "Steel Generals". The Pz.Bfw. VI (P) is an up-armoured variant of the VK 45.01 (P), with an extra 100 mm of armour bolted onto the front plate.
