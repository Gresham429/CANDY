Description of Pz.Sfl.Ic: 
The 5 cm Pak38 auf Pz.Kpfw.II Sonderfahrgestell 901 is a premium gift rank II German light tank 
with a battle rating of 2.3 (AB/RB/SB). It was introduced as a premium pack in Update 1.43 as the Pz.Kpfw. II Ausf. H (shortened to Pz.II H). In Update 1.89, the vehicle was renamed to the current designation. It was purchasable from the store until the 2021 May sale after which it was removed from sale. It has since been made available for purchase in-game with Golden Eagles  temporarily for the 2021, 2022, and 2023 "German Unity Day" mini-events.
