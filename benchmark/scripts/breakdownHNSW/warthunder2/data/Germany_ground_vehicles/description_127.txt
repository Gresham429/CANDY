Description of TAM_2IP: 
The Tanque Argentino Mediano 2IP is a premium rank VI German light tank 
with a battle rating of 9.0 (AB/RB/SB). It was introduced in Update "Red Skies". Sitting between the TAM and TAM 2C in terms of performance, this tank is essentially an earlier TAM model with access to more modern ammunition and add-on armour for increased protection.
