Description of Prendergast%27s_Spitfire_FR_Mk_XIVe: 
Prendergast's Spitfire FR Mk XIVe is a premium gift rank IV British fighter 
with a battle rating of 5.3 (AB/SB) and 5.7 (RB). It was introduced as a premium pack in Update 1.49 "Weapons of Victory" and was removed from the store after the 2018 Independence Day Summer Sale. It has been made available again for the 2018 "Royal Air Force Centenary", the 2020 "80th Anniversary of the Battle of Britain" events, and the 2021 May sale. It has also been available in-game for Golden Eagles  for a limited time during the 2021 and 2022 "Battle of Britain Day" mini-events.
