Description of Beaufort_Mk_VIII: 
The Beaufort Mk VIII is a rank I British bomber 
with a battle rating of 1.3 (AB), 1.7 (RB), and 2.0 (SB). It has been in the game since the start of the Open Beta Test prior to Update 1.27. Though the plane is in the British tech tree, it is painted with Australian markings.
