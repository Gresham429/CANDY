Description of Boomerang_Mk_II_(Great_Britain): 
The ▄Boomerang Mk II is a premium rank II British fighter 
with a battle rating of 3.3 (AB), 3.0 (RB), and 2.7 (SB). It has been in the game since the start of the Open Beta Test prior to Update 1.27 as a premium vehicle purchasable in-game with Golden Eagles , but was removed from in-game sale after the 9th Anniversary Sale. Buying the plane required the purchase of the Boomerang Mk I to unlock. It has been made available for Golden Eagles  during the 2022, 2023, and 2024 "Australia Day" mini-events. This variant of the Boomerang represents a version which was designed and manufactured in Australia.
