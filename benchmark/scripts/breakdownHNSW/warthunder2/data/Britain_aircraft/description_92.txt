Description of MB.5: 
The MB.5 is a premium rank IV British fighter 
with a battle rating of 5.0 (AB/RB) and 4.7 (SB). It was introduced as a premium pack in Update 1.77 "Advancing Storm". It was removed from the store after the 2019 Victory Day Sale, but was reintroduced briefly for the 2018 "Royal Air Force Centenary" and purchasable in-game with Golden Eagles  for War Thunder's 7th Anniversary.
