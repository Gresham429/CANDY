Description of Tuck%27s_Gladiator_Mk_II: 
The Tuck's Gladiator Mk II is a premium rank I British fighter 
with a battle rating of 1.3 (AB/SB) and 1.0 (RB). It was introduced in Update 1.31. The plane is painted with the camouflage scheme of British pilot Robert S. Tuck who served in the RAF with No.65 Squadron during the interwar period.
