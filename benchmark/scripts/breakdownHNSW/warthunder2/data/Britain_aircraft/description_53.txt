Description of Boomerang_Mk_I_(Great_Britain): 
The ▄Boomerang Mk I is a premium rank II British fighter 
with a battle rating of 3.3 (AB), 3.0 (RB), and 2.7 (SB). It has been in the game since the start of the Open Beta Test prior to Update 1.27. This variant of the Boomerang represents a version which was designed and manufactured in Australia.
