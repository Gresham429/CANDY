Description of Spitfire_F_Mk_24: 
The Spitfire F Mk 24 was the last production model of the famous Supermarine Spitfire. A total of 54 Mk 24 were manufactured, with 27 rebuilt from Mk 22 models. The Mk 24 was largely similar to the previous Mk 22, with the exception of having two rear fuselage fuel tanks installed as well as different rocket mountings allowing for installation of up to eight RP-3 rockets as opposed to six on the Mk 22. The main armament also differed, as the Mk 24 was armed with four 20 mm Hispano Mk.V cannons as opposed to Mk.II on the Spitfire Mk 22.
