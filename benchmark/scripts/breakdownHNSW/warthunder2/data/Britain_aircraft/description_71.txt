Description of Hellcat_Mk_II_(Great_Britain): 
The ▄Hellcat Mk II is a premium rank III British fighter 
with a battle rating of 3.0 (AB), 3.3 (RB), and 3.7 (SB). It has been in the game since the start of the Open Beta Test prior to Update 1.27 as the Hellcat Mk I, but was renamed to the Hellcat Mk II in Update 1.65 "Way of the Samurai".
