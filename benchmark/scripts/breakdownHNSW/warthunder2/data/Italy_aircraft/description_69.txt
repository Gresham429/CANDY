Description of G.91_R/4: 
The G.91 R/4 is a premium rank V Italian jet fighter 
with a battle rating of 8.3 (AB), 8.7 (RB), and 9.0 (SB). It was introduced in Update 1.87 "Locked On" as a pack vehicle purchasable in the Gaijin Store, but was removed from sale after the 2021 May Sale. It later returned for purchase for Golden Eagles  in Update "Red Skies".
