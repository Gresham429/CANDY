Description of Hs_129_B-2_(Romania)_(Italy): 
The Hs 129 B-2 (Romanian Air Force) is a premium rank III Italian strike aircraft 
with a battle rating of 3.3 (AB), 2.3 (RB), and 2.7 (SB). It was introduced as a premium pack vehicle in Update 1.83 "Masters of the Sea" for War Thunder's 6th Anniversary as a replacement for the version of the vehicle in the German tech tree.
