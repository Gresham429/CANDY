Description of SB_2M-103U_(China): 
The ␗SB 2M-103U is a rank II Chinese bomber 
with a battle rating of 2.7 (AB) and 2.3 (RB/SB). It was introduced in Update 1.91 "Night Vision". The Tupolev SB 20-103U is a mid 30's tactical bomber and is used as such in the game to strike bases or ground targets.
