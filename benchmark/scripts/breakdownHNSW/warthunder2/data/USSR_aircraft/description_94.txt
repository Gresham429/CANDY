Description of IL-2M_%22Avenger%22: 
The IL-2M "Avenger" is a premium gift rank III Soviet strike aircraft 
with a battle rating of 3.3 (AB/RB/SB). It was introduced as a premium pack in Update 1.41.27. It was removed from the store after the 2016 clearance sale. It was again available briefly in the store during the 2018 Winter Sale and has since been made available in-game for Golden Eagles () for a limited time during the 2018, 2020, 2021, 2022 "Defender of the Fatherland", and the 2023 "Defender Special" mini-events.
