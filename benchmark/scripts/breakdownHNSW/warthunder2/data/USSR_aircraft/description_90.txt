Description of P-39Q-15_(USSR): 
The ▂P-39Q-15 Airacobra is a premium gift rank III Soviet fighter 
with a battle rating of 3.3 (AB/RB/SB). It has been in the game since the start of the Open Beta Test prior to Update 1.27. It was gifted to players who participated in the alpha test for a year or more.
