Description of I-16_type_27: 
The I-16 type 27 Ishak is a rank II Soviet fighter 
with a battle rating of 3.3 (AB/RB) and 2.7 (SB). It was introduced in Update 1.39. It is the final development of the I-16, and it has the best set of weapons and ordnance available compared to the rest of the I-16s.
