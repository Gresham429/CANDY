Description of Golovachev%27s_Yak-9M: 
Golovachev's Yak-9M is a premium gift rank III Soviet fighter 
with a battle rating of 3.7 (AB/RB/SB). It was introduced as a premium pack in Update 1.49 "Weapons of Victory" and was removed from the Gaijin store in May 2016. It has since been brought back on occasion, such as the 2017 "Special Discounts!", the 2021 May sale, and the 2022 May sale.
