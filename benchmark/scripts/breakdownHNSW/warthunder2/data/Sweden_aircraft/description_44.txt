Description of J29D: 
The J29D Tunnan is a premium gift rank V Swedish jet fighter 
with a battle rating of 9.0 (AB) and 8.3 (RB/SB). It was introduced as a premium pack in Update 1.93 "Shark Attack" and was removed from the store after the 9th Anniversary Sale. It was briefly made available for purchase with Golden Eagles  for the 2023 "Swedish Air Force Day" mini-event.
