Description of Ki-10-II: 
The Ki-10-II, designated as Type 95 Fighter (九五式戦闘機), was an upgrade of the Ki-10-I with a stronger engine, a new cowling, a bigger rudder, and a stronger landing gear, making it faster, more agile, and more stable. The new engine increased the speed from 400 km/h (249 mph) to 430 km/h (267 mph) and the climb rate from 11.7 m/s (2,300 ft/min) to 13.3 m/s (2,620 ft/min). The cowling, rudder, landing gear, and wings were modified to fit the new engine and improve performance.
