Description of N1K2-Ja: 
The N1K2-Ja, designated as Shiden Kai Mod. 21 Ko (紫電改二一型甲), was a small scale modification of the N1K2-J which increased the bomb load capacity to four 60 kg bombs or two 250 kg bombs, and reduced the area of the vertical stabilizer by 13% by cutting the leading edge which improved the balance of manoeuvrability and stability.
