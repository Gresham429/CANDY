Description of Ki-10-I_C: 
This Ki-10-I (Commander), designated as Type 95 Fighter (九五式戦闘機), was assigned to the 1st Hiko Sentai, which was established on July 5, 1938 as the first flying unit in the Japanese Army Air Force. The 1st Hiko Sentai operated the Ki-10-I until May 1942, when it switched to the Ki-27. The Ki-10-I was then retired to training and secondary missions.
