Description of B6N2a_Model_12Ko: 
The B6N2a Model 12A, designated as Tenzan Mod.12 Ko (天山一二甲型), was a minor variant of the standard Tenzan 12 that replaced the two Type 92 (7.7 mm) LMGs with the German-inspired 13 mm Type 2 and 7.92 mm Type 1 guns, derived from the MG 131 and MG 15 respectively. It also had an H-6 (Type 3 Ku 6 Mod.4) air-to-surface naval Yagi-antenna radar mounted on the wing leading edges and on the sides of the rear fuselage.
